About THE SYMMETRY PROJECT

with One-on-One coaching sessions

The Symmetry Project is a sensible training system that focuses on redefining your body composition.

Feel Stronger

with One-on-One coaching sessions

One-on-one coaching sessions followed by a 100% personalized training system. An initial movement assessment will verify your start pointing and gives us the key areas of focus. From there, we create an individual strength and conditioning program for muscle hypertrophy and fat loss achieving your ideal body composition.

Feel Healthier

with a flexible diet plan

The essence of a good nutrition plan is building a healthy relationship with food. An optimal, yet flexible diet must be based on using all sources of food to fuel and energize your body. We will provide a simple macro-based diet with tools to help you track your daily caloric intake and your progress.

Feel Better

with balanced micronutrition

Vitamins and minerals matter. Deficiencies can negatively impact your overall health and lifestyle. We will assess your current micronutrient intake and provide a simple supplement plan.
