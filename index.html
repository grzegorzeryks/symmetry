BIO

Lukas Strankowski

NPC Classic Physique Champion
Strength and Conditioning Coach
Born: Warsaw, Poland
Located: Miami, FL

For more than 12 years, Lukas has been changing people’s lives by bringing together the best of Eastern Europe’s strength and conditioning techniques with a fresh, modern approach to nutrition and supplements. Coaching clients in Europe, Canada and United States inspiring people to make real changes in their fitness and lifestyle, Lukas created The Symmetry Training System with the sole goal of making people feel stronger, healthier and better.
