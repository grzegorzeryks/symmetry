BIO

Lukas Strankowski

NPC Classic Physique Champion
Strength and Conditioning Coach
Born: Warsaw, Poland
Located: Miami, FL

For more than 12 years, Lukas has been changing people’s lives by bringing together the best of Eastern Europe’s strength and conditioning techniques with a fresh, modern approach to nutrition and supplements. Coaching clients in Europe, Canada and United States inspiring people to make real changes in their fitness and lifestyle, Lukas created The Symmetry Training System with the sole goal of making people feel stronger, healthier and better.

Feel Healthier with a flexible diet plan
The essence of a good nutrition plan is building a healthy relationship with food. An optimal, yet flexible diet must be based on using all sources of food to fuel and energize your body. We will provide a simple macro-based diet with tools to help you track your daily caloric intake and your progress.
Feel Better with balanced micronutrition
Vitamins and minerals matter. Deficiencies can negatively impact your overall health and lifestyle. We will assess your current micronutrient intake and provide a simple supplement plan.
